My Weekend Hobbies

My name is Juan. On weekends, I like to do many different things. Usually, I wake up late on Saturday morning. After breakfast, I like to watch TV or listen to music.

Sometimes, I meet my friends at the park. We play football or just walk and talk. On Sunday, I usually read a book or help my parents at home.

I really enjoy my weekends because I can relax and do the things I love. My favorite hobby is listening to music. It makes me happy and calm.
